What is Ikigai?

Ikigai is a Japanese concept representing your reason for being. It's the sweet spot where:

  • Passion: What you love doing.
  • Mission: What the world needs.
  • Vocation: What you can be paid for.
  • Profession: What you're good at.

Finding your Ikigai can lead to a more fulfilling and meaningful life.

Finding Your Ikigai

This is a journey of self-discovery. Consider these steps:

  1. Reflect on your passions.
  2. Identify your skills and talents.
  3. Explore the world's needs.
  4. Consider your vocation (what you can be paid for).
  5. Visualize the intersection of these elements.
